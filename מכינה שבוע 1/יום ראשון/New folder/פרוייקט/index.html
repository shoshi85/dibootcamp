General Information

Nesta Robert Marley, known as Bob Marley, was a Jamaican musician and political activist. Marley is considered the one who brought the Rastafari religion into the world consciousness. Marley is the most famous reggae singer in the world, who has received recognition and many awards for his work. After his death, a number of compilation albums and cover version albums of his songs were released, some of which even reached the first places in the various music charts

  • Date of birth : February 6, 1945, Nine Miles, Jamaica
  • Date/place of death : May 11, 1981, Jackson Memorial Hospital, Miami, Florida, United States

Family
    Bob Marley married Alpharita Constantia "Rita" Anderson in Kingston, Jamaica, on 10 February 1966. Marley had many children: three were born to his wife Rita, and two additional children were adopted from Rita's previous relationships as his own, and they have the Marley name.
